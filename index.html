Here are some of the most common questions that we get:
What is Coffee Fruit?
Coffee fruit, or the coffee cherry, is the fruit that surrounds the coffee bean. It's a superfood packed with beneficial nutrients, including natural caffeine
Why have I not heard of Coffee Fruit before?
The coffee cherry offers numerous health benefits, including weight loss support, anti-aging properties, with a natural energy boost and used in many ancient cultures and also enjoyed locally. Unfortunately, it has a short shelf life once harvested for coffee and catches mold. Until we found a safe and hygienic way to bring you its benefits
Is Coffee Fruit Edible?
Yes, coffee fruit is edible and is used in our range of products for its health benefits
Coffee Fruit vs. Coffee Bean?
While the coffee bean is well-known for its use in making coffee, the surrounding fruit contains a wealth of nutrients that offer various health benefits
What are Coffee fruit energy bars?
Coffee fruit energy bars are delicious and nutritious snacks made from the fruit of the coffee plant. They provide a natural boost of energy and are packed with vitamins and minerals
What is Coffee fruit water?
Coffee fruit sparkling water is a refreshing beverage made from the fruit of the Coffee plant. It is carbonated and infused with the natural flavors of Coffee fruit, providing a unique and invigorating taste (Coming soon!)
What are Coffee fruit supplements?
Coffee fruit supplements are convenient and portable vegan capsules made from the extract of Coffee fruit. They are designed to support overall health and well-being, providing a concentrated dose of beneficial nutrients
How often can I have Coffee fruit extract?
Coffee fruit extract powder can be easily incorporated into your daily routine. Simply mix the desired amount of powder into water, smoothies, or other beverages. It can also be used in cooking and baking to add a nutritious twist to your favorite recipes
Are these products suitable for vegans?
Yes, all our Coffee fruit products are vegan-friendly. They are made from plant-based ingredients and do not contain any animal-derived components. Enjoy the goodness of Coffee fruit while following a vegan lifestyle
